Aeroplane Green Shuttles

The Aeroplane Green Label G1130 Shuttlecocks are renowned for their exceptional quality, consistency, and durability, making them a top choice for competitive and professional badminton players. Made from premium goose feathers, these shuttlecocks offer an accurate and stable flight trajectory, ensuring reliable performance during high-level gameplay.

The base of the shuttlecock is constructed from high-quality natural cork, which provides superior responsiveness and a satisfying feel upon impact. The feathers are carefully selected for uniformity, contributing to consistent speed and flight characteristics over extended periods of play.

Key Features:

  • Durability: Known for excellent durability, the shuttlecocks maintain their flight characteristics for longer compared to lower-grade shuttlecocks.
  • Flight Consistency: Offers superior flight stability and accurate trajectory, making it ideal for professional and tournament use.
  • Speed Options: Allowing players to choose based on their local climate or personal preference. Speed 77 and 78 are the most commonly used.
  • Performance: Suitable for advanced and professional players who demand precision and high performance from their shuttlecocks.

Product Details:

  • Model: G1130 (Green Label)
  • Material: Feathers - Premium goose feathers for superior flight and durability.
  • Flight Grade: Grade A- Suitable for tournament-level play and professional players.
  • Speed Options: Available in speeds 76, 77, 78, and 79.
  • Packaging: Typically sold in tubes of 12 shuttlecocks.
